Ad Window Extension

Welcome to Ad Window Extension! This browser extension displays a non-intrusive 300x250 ad banner in the corner of web pages. Users can close the ad window at any time, ensuring a seamless browsing experience.

Privacy Policy

Ad Window Extension does not collect personal data. Advertisements are served through third-party networks (e.g., Monetag) and may use cookies for ad targeting. You can close the ad window at any time.

About

This extension is designed to provide a lightweight and user-friendly way to display ads without disrupting your browsing.
